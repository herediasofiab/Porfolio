Legal Document AI Assistants

In this project, I developed intelligent assistants using the OpenAI agents platform and JSON processing. The agents processed legal documents such as wills, IDs, and deeds, extracting their textual content and structuring it in JSON format. Then, another assistant unified the data to generate an inheritance summary, identifying heirs and their respective inheritance percentages.

Due to data privacy policies, I cannot share the documents or source code.

Bank Transaction Processing Automation

I designed an automated flow with Power Automate to process bank receipts received by email. The system automatically extracted relevant data (amount, date, transaction type), logged it into an Excel sheet, and sent a processing confirmation email. Additionally, I trained AI agents specialized in interpreting different formats, currencies, and languages of bank receipts internationally, for both bond and stock transactions.

Order Automation with MAKE & Trello

I used the MAKE platform to automate order registration. Every time an order arrived via form, the system identified it and automatically created a new Trello card, assigning a responsible person, due date, and category according to configured rules. This solution centralized and visualized the order flow in real time without manual intervention.
